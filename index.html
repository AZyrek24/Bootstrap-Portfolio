About Me

Hello, my name is Tony Lee Zyrek and I am a student at the University of Arizona Coding Bootcamp, based in Tucson, AZ. I am learning to code as a full-stack web developer, and I am excited for the challenges ahead. My goals are to have done work in software development, front-end/back-end web, database/server management, mobile app development, video game development, and artificial intelligence.

I believe that a strong website demonstrates ideas; a place where I feel I can separate myself from others developers. I love to compose and play music, and I am a fan of the arts. Half of the reason that I like to golf is because I appreciate the landscape. The other half is continuing to improve at a life-long sport. It is with this mentality that I can continue to bring fresh ideas and compelling designs to an ever-growing internet.
